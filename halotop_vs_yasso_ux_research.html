Brand verdicts

Halo Top

The calorie king — 80 cal/serving is unbeaten in the category. Wins on strict dieters and keto-adjacent consumers. Loses significant points on texture (described as "icy" or "chalky" without optimal freezer temps) and on the erythritol aftertaste, which drives away casual users.

Yasso

The taste and texture winner. Greek yogurt base drives both health perception and real-food ingredient trust. Higher calorie count is its main weakness among strict calorie-counters, but fitness-focused consumers rate it higher on protein satisfaction. Strong in the 26–35 millennial sweet spot.

Who should buy Halo Top

Strict calorie trackers, keto-curious consumers, or anyone who wants to eat a full pint guilt-free. Flavor variety is a genuine strength — 30+ options beats Yasso's lineup.

Who should buy Yasso

Protein-conscious gym-goers, consumers wary of artificial sweeteners, and anyone who found Halo Top's texture disappointing. The bar format also offers better portion control than a pint.
