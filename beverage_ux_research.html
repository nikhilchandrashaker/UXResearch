Brand-level insights

Olipop

Dominates health perception (9.2/10) and packaging design. Consumers see it as a "functional treat." Weaker value scores signal premium pricing friction.

Poppi

Strong health perception (8.7) and millennial brand affinity. Social media presence inflates brand sentiment. Close second to Olipop in nearly every dimension.

Coke / Pepsi Zero

Highest taste satisfaction (8.3) among all segments. However, health perception scores are low (5.1). Massive distribution boosts value-for-money perception.

Sparkling Ice

Best value-for-money rating (7.9). Seen as a budget-friendly middle ground. Health perception is modest; packaging is considered dated by younger consumers.

Diet Coke / Pepsi

Lowest health perception (4.2) — aspartame concerns persist. Strong nostalgia lifts brand sentiment in 35–45 age cohort despite declining overall category interest.

Key finding

Health-forward brands (Olipop, Poppi) are winning brand sentiment & perception wars despite lower value scores. Legacy diet sodas must close the health story gap to remain competitive.
