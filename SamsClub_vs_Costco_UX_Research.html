Where each retailer falls short.

Costco

06 — Recommendations

What each retailer should do next.

For Sam's Club
01Expand Scan & Go to all member tiers — don't gate your best feature behind Plus
02Invest in in-store sensory experience — food court and sampling to rival Costco culture
03Improve fresh produce and deli quality perception through better private-label packaging
04Standardize store layout across locations to reduce friction for frequent shoppers
For Costco
01Prioritize self-checkout expansion — the single highest-impact UX improvement available
02Redesign website navigation — simplify categories, improve search, reduce density
03Develop a transactional mobile app with Scan & Go or pre-order capabilities
04Explore digital receipt alternatives (QR code) to eliminate the exit friction point
07 — Conclusion

The retailer that closes these gaps first
wins the next decade.

Sam's Club wins on digital. Costco wins on experience. A hybrid strategy — combining Costco's in-store delight with Sam's Club's digital efficiency — would represent true best-in-class warehouse retail UX.
