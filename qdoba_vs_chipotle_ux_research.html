Brand verdicts

Chipotle

Wins on ingredient quality, brand trust, and consistency. The "Food with Integrity" positioning resonates deeply with health-conscious consumers. Protein quality — especially chicken and carnitas — leads category in blind tests. Loses points on queso and menu variety.

Qdoba

Wins decisively on queso (9.1), menu variety, and free guacamole policy. Unique proteins like smoked brisket differentiate it meaningfully. Struggles to match Chipotle's brand equity and ingredient trust story despite comparable food quality.

Choose Chipotle if...

You prioritize ingredient sourcing, consistent protein quality, and brand reputation. Bigger portions tip the value equation in Chipotle's favor for heavy eaters, even at similar base prices.

Choose Qdoba if...

You love guac on everything (free adds up fast), want queso that actually slaps, or crave menu variety like smoked brisket or breakfast. Also the regional favorite in Colorado, where it edges Chipotle in location density.
